About

At Bloomington Roofing Company, we provide a full service New roof, re-roof, commercial roof, and repairs to homeowners and Building owners throughout Indiana. Licensed and Skilled, our roofers combine the latest technology, highest quality, and most advanced techniques with industry-leading client recommended service.

Whether you need a new roof, roof maintenance, or roof repairs for Shingle, metal, tile, wood shake, composite shake, rubber, or TPO, you can rely on our team of professionals at Bloomington Roofing Company to get the job done.

We also are proud to provide energy-efficient green roofs to help you save money while going green. Whatever the roofing project, we deliver top quality craftsmanship and results with integrity.

We are committed to relationships with clients that are based on mutual trust and respect.
